LADISLAV GRADEČAK

Independent researcher in philosophy of mind and consciousness

Biography

Ladislav Gradečak is an independent researcher whose work explores the intersection of mathematics, phenomenology, and cybernetics. He is the founder of Xrealism, a philosophical and scientific framework aimed at modeling the quintic resonance structure of perception and consciousness.

Gradečak's research introduces original diagrams, symbolic operators, and functional models such as the Conscioperceptual Unit (CPUₙ), the Quintic Spiral of Consciousness, and the Integral of Presence. His work emphasizes non-reductionist approaches to the study of consciousness, highlighting dynamic interaction between perception (Πₙ), awareness (Ψₙ), attention (𝐀⃗), and silence (𝕊).

He first presented the theoretical basis of Xrealism in 2011 in the essay XREALISM (ISBN: 978-953-56881-1-2, Miniprint, Varaždin), and registered the concept as intellectual property in 2012. His scientific contributions are openly available under Creative Commons licenses via platforms such as Zenodo and ResearchGate.

Key areas of interest include: quintic energy dynamics, resonant integrals, attention-perception coupling, ontological modulation, and phenomenological modeling of consciousness structures.

About Xrealism
